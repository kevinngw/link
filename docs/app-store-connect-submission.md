## Suggested description

Link Pulse is a fast mobile board for Seattle-area transit riders who want live arrivals without digging through multiple apps or station pages.

Use Link Pulse to:

- Check live train positions and active service status across the system
- Open any station and see the next realtime arrivals in each direction
- Save favorite stations for quick access
- Browse line-level insights, delays, and alerts
- Find nearby stations when you choose to share your location
- Switch between English and Chinese inside the app

Link Pulse currently focuses on Link light rail, RapidRide, and Swift, with realtime boards designed for quick checks while commuting.

Location access is optional and only used when you trigger nearby station search. Favorites, recent stations, theme, and language preferences stay on your device.
